KidsHavingFun (KHF) is an independent collective of artists, skaters, hippies, and misfits, with a focus on promoting a philosophy of enjoying the simpler things through different vehicles. We use art and skateboarding as a means to bring social change. Whether it's a live event, charitable services, or culturally inspred street wear. We create dope shit. KHF to us is not about being a child. It is about finding and doing what you enjoy. Progression is key, and we are glad to be a part of that journey with you. HAVE FUN...
